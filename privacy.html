Twitter Privacy Policy

Last updated November 26, 2022

The Republic of Melonia created and maintain official accounts on Twitter to better engage with the Melonia public. Activity on Twitter is governed by Twitter's privacy policy. Consequently, you should review the privacy policies of Twitter before using it to ensure an understanding of how your information may be used. You should also adjust the privacy settings of your account on Twitter to match your preferences.

The Republic of Melonia may archive information that users send when engaging with any of its accounts (e.g., by sending a direct message, Tweeting a reply, following an account, etc).
